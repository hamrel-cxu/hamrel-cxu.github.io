Chen Xu

Research Scientist, Toyota Research Institute

I work on safe and robust Large Behavior Models (i.e., generalist policies) for robot manipulation at the Toyota Research Institute (Robotics division), on the Trustworthy Learning under Uncertainty (TLU) team.

Research interests. safe & reliable generalist manipulation policies, flow-based generative modeling, uncertainty quantification.

Education. PhD in Operations Research (specializing in Machine Learning) with Prof. Yao Xie at Georgia Tech ISyE. Before that, joint BS/MS degrees with Prof. Rina Foygel Barber at the University of Chicago — MS in Statistics, BS in Computational & Applied Mathematics and Economics (data science concentration).
